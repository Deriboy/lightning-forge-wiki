About

Lightning Forge is a Norse Mythology themed Minecraft mod that adds a way to craft useful and powerful items using the power of lightning. This website serves as an official documentation of the mod for its users.

Getting Started

During exploration, you may come across ancient artifact fragments. These fragments can be used to reforge powerful magical artifacts such as the Prospector's Pickaxe. To get started, you need to use fulgurite to build a lightning forge. Fulgurite, also known as petrified lightning, can be found where lightning strikes sandy areas.
