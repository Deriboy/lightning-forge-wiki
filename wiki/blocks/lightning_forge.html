The Lightning Forge

Traditional forges are not powerful enough to reforge ancient powerful artifacts. The lighting forge harnesses the power of the gods to renew the magic in the artifacts of old.

To craft an item using the forge, put each of the ingredients in any of the four corners of the forge, and wait for lightning to strike it during a thunderstorm.

Rather than relying on random chance to strike the forge, lightning rods should be used to attract and channel the energy of the lightning. Various materials make for better or worse rod materials. Lightning rods can be as tall as you wish, though be warned that the benefit of making a lightning rod taller diminishes quickly.
